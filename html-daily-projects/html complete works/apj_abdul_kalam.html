Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | Former President of India

Biography

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He playd a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missle development efforts, earning him the title "Missile Man of India." His life continues to inspire of students and professionals across the world.
